About Me

Hi I am Neildor and I am a lecturer at my local university teaching health sciences. I am a digital enthusiast and this manifests itself in my enthusiasm towards technology enhanced learning. I have been working in partnership with the development team at Microsoft to provide feedback for Microsoft Teams in terms of its use as a higher education virtual learning environment, and this has been very successful so far.

Over the last few years i have been trying to increase my own digital literacy, which started off being relational database software, namely access. Since then i have developed an interest in manipulating data, which led me to Pyhton. I have been learning the ropes of python for about 18 months and have a reasonable grasp of it's core concepts. My latest interest is, whilst still including all of the above, webdevelopment. as this will provide a great tool for disseminating key course information to my students, in a ubiquitous environment, whilst simultaneously addressing accessibility needs.
